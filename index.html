About the maker

R avir Scott — born Ravish Kumar — is from Sonma, Begusarai, in Bihar, India. He is an Artist, Developer and Author, known for his work as an independent music artist, for developing tech, and for writing books. His work is entirely self-made — no record label, no production house, no co-writers.

He builds websites, records music, writes books, and experiments with technology — continuously learning and creating on his own terms.
